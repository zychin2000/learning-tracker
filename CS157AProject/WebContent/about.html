OUR PRINCIPLES OF LEARNING

Our seven principles of learning come from a perspective that is developmental and holistic. In other words, we begin with the recognition that learning is a developmental process that intersects with other developmental processes in a student ’ s life.

students enter our classrooms not only with skills, knowledge, and abilities, but also with social and emotional experiences that infl uence what they value, how they perceive themselves and others, and how they will engage in the learning process. Consistent with this holistic perspective, readers should understand that, although we address each principle individually to highlight particular issues pertaining to student learning, they are all at work in real learning situations and are functionally inseparable.
